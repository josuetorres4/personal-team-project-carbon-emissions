# Opportunity Framing

## Existing Solutions

### PlanA.Earth

PlanA.Earth provides a carbon management platform that helps organizations measure, manage, and reduce their greenhouse gas emissions. The platform focuses on carbon accounting, sustainability analytics, and decarbonization planning. It helps companies track emissions across operations and develop strategies aligned with sustainability and ESG reporting frameworks.

PlanA supports organizations by collecting emissions data, generating reports, and helping teams identify areas where emissions can be reduced.

**Strengths**

- Comprehensive carbon accounting across organizational activities  
- Sustainability reporting and ESG compliance support  
- Decarbonization planning tools and emissions tracking  

**Limitations**

- Primarily focused on reporting and analysis rather than operational optimization  
- Limited automation for infrastructure-level changes such as cloud workload scheduling  
- Requires manual implementation of emissions reduction strategies  


---

### Normative.io

Normative.io provides enterprise carbon accounting software designed to help organizations measure, report, and reduce emissions across Scope 1, Scope 2, and Scope 3 categories. The platform emphasizes scientific accuracy and alignment with international climate reporting standards.

Normative integrates organizational data to identify emissions hotspots and provide recommendations for reduction strategies.

**Strengths**

- Accurate carbon accounting aligned with established reporting standards  
- Data integration and emissions hotspot analysis  
- Strong support for corporate sustainability reporting  

**Limitations**

- Focused primarily on measurement and reporting  
- Optimization strategies are largely advisory rather than automated  
- Does not actively adjust operational systems such as cloud workloads in response to carbon signals  


---

## Identified Gap

Both PlanA and Normative provide strong carbon accounting and sustainability reporting capabilities. However, these platforms primarily function as analytics tools rather than systems that actively reduce emissions through operational changes.

Several key gaps exist:

1. **Lack of closed-loop optimization**  
   Existing platforms measure emissions but do not continuously optimize infrastructure or workloads to reduce them.

2. **Limited real-time response**  
   Carbon accounting systems often analyze historical data instead of responding dynamically to changing grid carbon intensity or workload demand.

3. **Manual implementation of recommendations**  
   Sustainability teams must manually interpret reports and implement recommended changes in infrastructure or operations.

4. **Limited verification of carbon reduction outcomes**  
   Many tools estimate potential emissions reductions but do not verify the actual impact of implemented changes.


---

## Opportunity for Our System

Our system addresses these gaps by introducing a **multi-agent optimization platform for cloud carbon management**.

Rather than functioning solely as a reporting tool, the system operates as a **closed-loop control system** that continuously improves cloud infrastructure decisions.

The system:

1. Collects cloud workload and infrastructure data  
2. Calculates emissions using deterministic carbon accounting methods  
3. Generates optimization recommendations for workload scheduling and region placement  
4. Executes approved changes through infrastructure automation  
5. Verifies the real-world impact of those changes using counterfactual analysis  

This approach transforms carbon management from **passive reporting into active operational optimization**.
