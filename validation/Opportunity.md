# Opportunity Framing

## Problem
Individuals attempting to understand their carbon footprint encounter tools that are inconsistent, difficult to verify, and poorly adapted to incomplete real-world data.

## Opportunity
Develop an AI powered system that produces transparent, explainable, and actionable carbon emissions insights.

---

## Product Requirements

### Transparency
- Display emissions factors and assumptions
- Show calculation steps
- Provide uncertainty ranges

### Data Intake
- Accept messy real world inputs
- Normalize bills and commute information
- Request only high-impact missing data

### Actionability
- Rank reduction actions by impact
- Provide realistic behavioral recommendations
- Track estimated emissions reductions

### Trust & Safety
- Prevent misleading sustainability claims
- Encourage evidence-based reporting

---

## Value Proposition
Our system moves beyond estimation toward trustworthy decision support for emissions reduction.
